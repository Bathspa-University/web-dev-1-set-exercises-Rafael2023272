Overview

Terminator 3: Rise of the Machines is a 2003 American science fiction action film directed by Jonathan Mostow and starring Arnold Schwarzenegger, Nick Stahl, Claire Danes, and Kristanna Loken. It is the third installment in the Terminator film series. In the film, Skynet sends a new Terminator, the T-X, back in time to eliminate John Connor's future lieutenants, including his future wife Kate Brewster, and a reprogrammed Terminator, the T-850, to protect them.

Plot

It's been 10 years since John Connor saved Earth from Judgment Day, and he's now living under the radar, steering clear of using anything Skynet can trace. That is, until he encounters T-X, a robotic assassin ordered to finish what T-1000 started. Good thing Connor's former nemesis, the Terminator, is back to aid the now-adult Connor just like he promised.
